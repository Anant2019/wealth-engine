Strategy Directive: Why are we choosing these specific assets?

Our mathematical solver explicitly maps your capital into targeted asset vehicles to optimize for both absolute safety and high yield based on duration:

  • Liquid & Safety Net (~7%): We use Savings, FDs, and Post Office schemes for absolute capital protection. These fund your ₹10k liquid buffer and your 6-month safety net.
  • Growth Equities (~12-15%): Your Mutual Funds, Stocks, and ULIPs act as the primary wealth engine. These are volatile but historically double your money every 4-6 years.
  • Shield Assets (~10%): Corporate Bonds and NPS tiers provide a cushion during market crashes, ensuring your entire portfolio doesn't drop to zero.
  • Hedge Assets (~8%): Gold, SGBs, and Real Estate preserve purchasing power against rising prices (inflation).
